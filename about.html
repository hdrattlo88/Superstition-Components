Community, Construction, Caring About Us

Started in Eloy, Arizona in 2018, Superstition Components L.L.C. not only builds great products, but brings a new and innovative solution to the ever growing recidivism problem in the state of Arizona. Superstition Components employs inmates within their manufacturing facility to built the ConTek line of sheds. This gives the inmates valuable hands-on skills like carpentry and drafting that they can use upon release to re-enter the work force. At Superstition, we believe that the more educated all of our community members are, the better. Here's to building a better, brighter Arizona tomorrow.
